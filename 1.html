Introduction to Football: 축구란 무엇인가?

Football is a sport in which 2 teams, each consisting of 11 players, compete to score as many goals as possible for the duration of 90 minutes.

The 11 players are allocated different positions that help the game to be played tactically. Beginning with the goalkeeper who can use hands to block an incoming ball to the goal post, there are the defenders, midfielders, and the strikers.
